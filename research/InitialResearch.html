Research Process

Taking notes based on useful articles that we've found.

Problem Statement

People are experiencing problems with handling their grief in healthy ways

Complicated grief is "characterized by prolonged acute grief”

Need Statement

Anyone, and probably everyone will go through grief. However, ways with dealing with grief can be unhealthy.

By improving mental health services (by increasing resources and increasing access) and encouraging people to help out the people around them, we can help fight complicated grief.

People can find ways that they can cope with loss that fit their needs and situation

  • “The result is delayed healing and increased pain which occurs because aspects of a person's response to the circumstances or consequences of the death derail the mourning process, interfering with learning, and preventing the natural healing process from progressing.” Shear, M Katherine. “Grief and mourning gone awry: pathway and course of complicated grief.” Dialogues in clinical neuroscience vol. 14,2 (2012): 119-28. doi:10.31887/DCNS.2012.14.2/mshear
  • “CG symptoms cause a great deal of distress and usually interfere with functioning and with the ability to find meaning and purpose in life… Many people with CG have suicidal thinking, sometimes at a level that is of concern.” Shear, M Katherine. “Grief and mourning gone awry: pathway and course of complicated grief.” Dialogues in clinical neuroscience vol. 14,2 (2012): 119-28. doi:10.31887/DCNS.2012.14.2/mshear
  • We need to educate people on how to notice unhealthy ways of coping so we can prevent others from going through it or prevent it from happening to ourselves.
